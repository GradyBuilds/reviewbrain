Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does ReviewBrain connect to my Google reviews?+
You sign in with your Google Business account and grant ReviewBrain read access. We pull in your reviews automatically. Setup takes less than 2 minutes and we walk you through every step.
How does the AI know what to write?+
Our AI reads the full review text, the star rating, and your business type. It crafts a response that matches the tone — warm and grateful for positive reviews, empathetic and solution-focused for negative ones. Every reply sounds natural, not robotic.
Can I edit the replies before sending?+
Absolutely. Every AI-generated reply is a suggestion. You can edit it however you want, add your own personal touch, or rewrite it entirely. Nothing gets posted without your approval.
What if I don't like the AI response?+
Just click "Regenerate" and the AI will write a completely new reply. You can regenerate as many times as you want until you're happy with it. You can also edit any response manually.
Do I need to be tech-savvy to use this?+
Not at all. If you can check your email and click a button, you can use ReviewBrain. The whole point is to make review management dead simple for busy business owners, not tech experts.
Does it work for Yelp and Facebook too?+
Yes! ReviewBrain pulls reviews from Google, Yelp, and Facebook all into one dashboard. You manage everything from a single screen instead of jumping between three different sites.
What happens after my free trial ends?+
After your 14-day free trial, you can continue for just $19/month. No contracts, cancel anytime. If you cancel, you won't be charged again. We also offer a money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.
How is this different from BrightLocal or Podium?+
Those tools are built for agencies managing 50+ locations and cost $44–$259/month. ReviewBrain is built specifically for single-location local businesses at a fraction of the price. Simple, focused, and actually affordable.
